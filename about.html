About Me

I’m an aspiring electrical engineer passionate about sustainable technologies and the Internet of Things.

Interests

  • Electric Vehicles
  • Renewable Energy
  • Internet of Things
  • Core Electrical Systems


As an aspiring electrical engineer, my passion lies in the intersection of hardware and software, particularly in how innovative technologies can transform industries. I am fascinated by the way electrical systems power the modern world and the role that engineering plays in creating sustainable, efficient solutions. From understanding core electrical systems to exploring renewable energy sources, I am eager to develop the skills necessary to contribute to the rapidly evolving field of electrical engineering. While my primary focus has always been on electrical engineering, I also have a deep interest in artificial intelligence (AI) and computer science. These domains are incredibly exciting because they hold the potential to revolutionize everything from consumer devices to industrial automation, and they also offer ways to enhance traditional engineering practices. Here's how I see the connection between electrical engineering, AI, and computer science: Electrical Engineering & AI: AI is increasingly being integrated into electrical systems and devices, such as in smart grids, electric vehicles, and automated control systems. Understanding both the hardware (electrical engineering) and the software (AI and algorithms) gives me a unique advantage in designing systems that are not only efficient but intelligent and adaptive to changing environments. Computer Science: While electrical engineering has been my primary focus, my curiosity about programming, data science, and machine learning drives me to explore the broader field of computer science. From designing algorithms that optimize electrical circuits to creating the software that powers embedded systems, computer science complements my engineering knowledge and helps me build solutions that are both practical and innovative. My goal is to bring a multidisciplinary approach to the problems of the future. Whether it’s developing AI-powered devices, contributing to the next generation of IoT (Internet of Things) applications, or working on sustainable energy systems, I’m excited about the opportunities to integrate my knowledge in electrical engineering with my growing skills in AI and computer science
